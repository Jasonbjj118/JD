Senior Account Management Lead, Uber Retail

Internal Job Description

This role sits within the Uber Retail (GR) Commercial team in Taiwan and plays a key role in accelerating the growth of Rest of Retail (RoR) as a strategic pillar for 2026.

The role will oversee a portfolio of retail partners and support a team of Account Managers organized by retail operating model (Category-led, Mission-driven, Moment-led, Routine Grocery). The focus will be on driving structured growth strategies by vertical, strengthening business fundamentals, and elevating commercial rigor across the team.

Key internal priorities include:

  1. Improving vertical-level strategy clarity and growth roadmaps
  2. Enhancing AM operating discipline and performance management
  3. Strengthening cross-functional collaboration with Ops, Ads, Commercial team
  4. Identifying the next curve of growth beyond store and SKU expansion

This is a high-impact leadership role for someone who can combine strategic thinking with strong execution discipline, and who is interested in shaping the long-term evolution of Uber Retail in Taiwan.

About the Role

The Uber Retail team is building the future of on-demand retail in Taiwan, helping merchants grow sustainably through the Uber Eats platform. This role leads a diverse portfolio of retail partners and a team, driving commercial strategy and business performance across multiple retail categories. You will directly influence marketplace growth, category expansion, and long-term partner success within a fast-evolving ecosystem.

What You'll Do

  • Lead and develop a team responsible for managing a portfolio of retail partners
  • Drive commercial performance by identifying growth opportunities and executing strategic initiatives
  • Build strong relationships with key retail partners and influence long-term business outcomes
  • Collaborate cross-functionally to improve marketplace metrics and operational excellence
  • Translate data insights into actionable plans that drive sustainable business growth

Basic Qualifications

  • 8+ years of experience in retail, FMCG, marketplace, or account management environments
  • Demonstrated experience influencing stakeholders, leading projects, or mentoring team members
  • Strong commercial acumen with a track record of driving revenue or growth metrics

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience managing national retail chains or multi-category portfolios
  • Experience working in a fast-paced, high-growth, or platform-based business environment
  • Strong data fluency with the ability to translate insights into commercial actions
  • Experience leading cross-functional initiatives across operations, marketing, or finance
  • Background in e-commerce, marketplace, or omnichannel retail models

Partner Manager, Retail - Taipei

About the Role

The Uber Eats team is seeking sharp, highly motivated individuals to join the Retail segment, managing some of our most strategic partners. As a Partner Manager, you will work closely with merchant partners to drive revenue growth, operational excellence, and customer experience. You will collaborate cross-functionally with local and central operations, product, sales, marketing, and engineering teams to optimize partnerships and deliver maximum value for both Uber and its merchant partners.

You will thrive in this role if you:

  • Perform at your best in a collaborative team environment
  • Demonstrate a strong appetite for continuous learning
  • Value ideas and impact over hierarchy
  • Are deeply customer-obsessed and results-driven

What You'll Do

  • Own the commercial relationship for assigned retail categories, managing multiple merchants through annual planning and regular weekly and monthly business reviews
  • Execute portfolio-level commercial strategies aligned with category and merchant-specific growth objectives
  • Partner closely with operations teams to ensure a high-quality consumer experience
  • Develop and implement action plans to improve key performance indicators, including tailored promotional strategies by category or specialty store
  • Present and recommend data-backed solutions to merchants, clearly articulating the value proposition of Ads and Direct offerings
  • Serve as the primary coordination point between merchants and internal teams to ensure accurate and timely execution

Basic Qualifications

  • Minimum 3 years of full-time professional experience in account management, retail, CPG, or a similar commercial role
  • Prior client-facing experience, ideally managing enterprise or mid-market accounts
  • Strong analytical capability with confidence in making data-driven decisions and applying sound business judgment
  • Demonstrated track record of managing complex partnerships and delivering measurable results

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience in Grocery, Retail, or CPG industries (preferred but not required)
  • Experience managing complex datasets and tracking and achieving multiple OKRS
  • Strong project management skills, including experience building joint business plans and delivering consistent performance outcomes
  • Demonstrated ability to operate effectively within a cross-functional organizational matrix
  • Experience partnering with Product and Engineering teams to surface insights and influence product improvements
